
Player Inputs:
--------------
- WASD || Arrows to move
- Mouse Left click to shoot bullets
- Mouse Right click to fire projectile

Player Attack
-------------
- Shooting normal
- Fired as projectile

Enemy Behavior
--------------
- Will follow or jump within the required range 
- You can reset time to spawn enemy again

Environment
-----------
- You can setup the size of the environment (*from unity not json file)
